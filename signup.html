Membership

Membership is open to anyone that is interested in woodworking no matter your age or skill level. The benefits of membership are greatest for those that actively participate in all of the activities of the Northwest Woodworkers Association.

To be a regular member, just sign up for the forum and join in on the discussions, meetings, etc.

Supporting members support the club financially, and receive an official membership card which can be used for a dicsount at our sponsors. Annual dues are $20. In order to become a supporting member or to renew an existing membership, use the Pay Now button below. Once we have received your payment, we will send you a membership card and add you to the group mailing list. For questions, please contact the membership@nwwoodworkers.org.
